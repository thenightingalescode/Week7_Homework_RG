Parks & Recreation

The story of a little town that loves a minature pony, has a racoon problem and is home to the best parks and recreation dept around.

Meet the cast of characters:


Leslie Knope

Leslie Barbara Knope is Pawnee's biggest fan.

For most of the show's run, she serves as Deputy Director of the Parks and Recreation Department of the fictional city of Pawnee, Indiana. An over-achiever, Knope believes the government should serve the people.

She lives for her job in the city government, waffles from JJ's Diner and making all of her friend as happy as can be. She displays the photographs of Madeline Albright, Condoleezza Rice, Janet Reno, and Nancy Pelosi. Leslie is a proud member of numerous action committees in and around City Hall, including, but not limited to: Dick Lovers Anonymous, the Equal Opportunity Committee, the Fun in the Sun Committee, the Clean Restroom Task Force, the Increased Access to Restrooms Task Force, the Handicapped Restroom Task Force, the Committee for Repainting Handicapped Parking Spaces Task Force, and the Task Force to Reduce the Number of Public Restrooms.

Ron Swanson

Ronald Ulysses Swanson is Leslie Knope's superior, and the Director of the Pawnee City Parks and Recreation department. Ron is an everyman with simple tastes. He loves steak, liberty, the great outdoors, and women named Tammy (he marries two Tammy's during the show's run).

Ron's dislikes include the government, Canada, and skim milk, she he claims is simply "milk lying about being milk".

Ron Swanson also has an alter ego named Duke Silver, who plays Jazz Saxophone by night.

April Ludgate

April Ludgate is a former intern at the Pawnee City Parks Department. As an intern, she had access to the official website. This is her bio, according to her:

April Ludgate was born in Björk’s house in Iceland and grew up on Easter Island, where her parents were giant stone heads. She has the ability to fire beams of tacos out of her hands and she can turn her legs into tigers. On Sundays, April enjoys reading Family Circus and traveling through time. Her favorite color is greenish-transparent and her favorite movie is the one you just watched. April is in charge of uploading the staff bios to the website, and no one has checked over her work.

Ann Perkins

Ann Perkins is a nurse in Pawnee and also Leslie Knope's best friend. She has been called the following by Leslie over the course of the show:

"Oh, Ann, you beautiful, rule-breaking moth."

“Ann, you are such a good friend, you’re a beautiful, talented, brilliant, powerful musk-ox. Thank you, ox.”

“Ann, you cunning, pliable, chestnut-haired sunfish.”

“Ann, you poetic, noble land-mermaid.”

Andy Dwyer

Andy Dwyer is a musician, children's entertainer, and former shoeshine boy. In the beginning of the show, he was dating Ann Perkins, but ended up dating and marrying April Ludgate.

He is the former lead singer of a local Pawnee band called Mouse Rat, aka Scarecrow Boat, aka Fleetwood Mac Sex Pants, aka God Hates Figs, aka Threeskin, aka Fourskin, aka Fiveskin.

His alter egos include Johnny Karate, a singing children's entertainer, and Bert Macklin- FBI Agent.

He also once fell into a pit.

Tom Haverford

Tom Haverford is an Administrator at the Pawnee Parks and Recreation Department. He's also a savvy entrepreneur, having cofounded Entertainment 720, Rent-A-Swag, and Tom's Bistro.

He's known for creating the holiday "Treat Yo Self", his extensive collection of pocket squares, and asking of all songs "Is It a Banger?"

Donna Meagle

Donna Meagle is the long-time serving Office Manager of the Parks and Recreation department. She claims that she's been working there since before Leslie Knope, which is impressive.

Donna is a mysterious woman. We are never told THAT much about her, but we do know the following:

1) She LOVES her Mercedes.

2) Her cousin is the R&B Star Ginuwine (yes THE Ginuwine).

3) She loves to "Treat Yo Self" along with Tom.

4) She loves the TWILIGHT series.

Ben Wyatt

Ben Wyatt is a state auditor who was sent to Pawnee, Indiana to go over their records. Upon meeting Leslie Knope, the two fell in love, and were eventually married and had three children together.

Ben is infamous for once being elected mayor of his hometown at the age of 18. His inexperience and immaturity was his downfall, and after spending all of the towns money on a complex called "Ice Town" he was sent packing.

He is a self-professed lover of Calzones, the band Letters to Cleo, and nerdy board games (including the one he created, The Cones of Dunshire). He also hosted a radio program in college called "Zoot Suit Wyatt".

Chris Treager

Chris Traeger is-literally-the healthiest person in all of Pawnee. He was sent to Pawnee alongside Ben Wyatt as a state auditor, and he eventually fell in love with Ann Perkins.

Chris is a health-freak. He takes many types of supplements to stay healthy, and believes that these, along with his 2.8% body fat, will make him the first human being to live to be 150 years old.

He is the most cheerful and upbeat person in all of the Parks and Recreation gang.

Jerry Gergich

Jerry/Barry/Larry/Lenny/Barry/Gerry Gergich did a lot of stuff around the office, and he usually was pretty bad at it. No one really cares what he did. He had a heart attack once, and he lived to be 100 years old.

Fun Facts about Terry:

1) He used to be a park ranger

2) He uses the same diabetes medicine as Lil' Sebastian (RIP LIL SEBASTIAN YOU ARE FOREVER MISSED!)

3) Jerry was a frequent customer at Sherm's Ice Cream parlor in Muncie, where he drops his ice cream "about half the time."

4) He also has a knockout of a wife.
